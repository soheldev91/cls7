ABOUT US

S.E.M INVEST FUND AG is a highly specialized and 100% independent asset management company dedicated to providing personalized investment solutions to best suit your individual needs. Strong customer orientation is the main focus of our organization allowing us to deliver true value and reassurance to our clients.

With our extensive expertise in risk management, market analysis and financial trading, we cater to the needs of our global client base of private investors, high-net-worth individuals, banks and private trust funds. We kindly invite you to view our website in more detail to learn how you can benefit from our services.

Our asset management focusses on long-term asset class assessments rather than tactical maneuvering.

With this approach we feel well positioned thanks to our multi asset investment framework and are confident to be able to deliver what our stratgeies both in private asset management and investment fund management should provide: the appropriate, risk-adjusted returns our investors ask for. Consistent, reliable investment processes, a solid portfolio construction and dedicuated investment professionals are the key pillars for this.
